GET /services/object-drive/1.0/objects/11e9d5a5fca3c3deb4900242ac120005/stream HTTP/1.1

GET /services/object-drive/1.0/objects/11e9d5a5fcb3ff63b4900242ac120005/stream HTTP/1.1
Host: proxier:8080
